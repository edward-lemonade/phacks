- The argument is well-structured, with each section addressing a distinct, significant flaw.
- The examples used are clear and effectively illustrate the abstract concepts being criticized.
strength_reasoning: The thesis is supported by three distinct, well-argued lines of reasoning that are common and powerful critiques of this ethical framework.
