- "thesis": the central/root claim

- "evidence": a fact, statistic, or cited data point
- "axiom": an assumed truth or unstated premise
- "counterclaim": an objection, rebuttal, or fallacy-style attack on another claim.

- "supports": source supports target

- "fallacious": not necessarily false, but the reasoning offered for it in the text is flawed
- "false": logically or verifiably false statement in context

- counterarguments: 0–3 short strings — plausible objections and fallacy-style critiques here
